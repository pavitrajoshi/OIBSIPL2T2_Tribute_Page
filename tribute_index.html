Sudha Murty

Introduction

Sudha Murty is a renowned Indian author, social worker, and philanthropist. She is the chairperson of the Infosys Foundation and has played a significant role in various charitable and educational initiatives.

Education

Sudha Murty completed her Bachelor's degree in Electrical Engineering from the B.V.B. College of Engineering and Technology in Hubli, Karnataka, India. She later pursued a Master's degree in Computer Science from the Indian Institute of Science in Bangalore.

Key Achievements

  • Recipient of the Padma Shri award for her contributions to literature and social work.
  • Author of numerous books in English and Kannada, covering a wide range of genres.
  • Established several libraries and computer centers in rural areas.
